About Us

We students from AISSMS COE PUNE have designed website for connecting clubs to the students and providing an interactive platform with information of various clubs to the students. Various clubs are very important to motivate young artist and help them to develop their creative skills. Many students dont know the clubs name & their recruitment dates as well as clubs event date,time,place etc. We have designed this website with a view to bring all clubs information and their activities on one platform.
